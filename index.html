My Journey:

Middle School - My artistic journey began in middle school (7-8th Grade). I was accepted into my school district's fine arts accademy known as the North Valley Arts Accademy. Here, I began Learning the basics for art. - I was introduced to new vocabulary, mediums, and art styles. The assignments I did in class were structured and planned by my teacher, who was wonderful at teaching us individually!

High School - Once I entered high school, I began to build off of the artistic foundation I had gained in middle school. Now, I was given more artistic liberty in class, often times deciding what projects to take on in my preferred medium. It was during these four years that I developed my love for acrylic paint. I also learned to enjoy painting landscapes based off of photos I had taken. By photographing my own reference photos, I was able to claim full ownership of my final paintings.
